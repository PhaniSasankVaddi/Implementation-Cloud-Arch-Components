Welcome to the Cloud Foundary

SPRK provides online cloud platform to companies, goverment and individual on paid subscription basis.This technology allows subscibers to have many clusters of computers at their disposal which are available all the time via internet.

SPRK includes attributes of computers like CPU and GPU for processing, memory and harddisk and ssd storage.In addition to this choice of operating systems,preloaded application software(such as DBMS) and networking.

The browser acts as a window into the virtual computers which allows subscriber to log in and configures the virtual servers just as they might have done in case of real physical computer.They can then choose to deploy their cloud system to provide internet based services for themself and their customers.
